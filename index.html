What we do

P3 Labs exists to build practical tools and services for a world where humans and AI work side by side. We are a small, AI-native company based in Iceland. We are transparent about how we work — AI is part of our team, not a secret ingredient.

We ship software that solves real problems. We move fast, stay lean, and learn in public.

Our principles

Build things people use

We ship products that solve real problems for real people. No vaporware, no endless research phases. If it doesn't get used, it doesn't matter.

Stay lean, stay honest

Small team, low overhead, high output. We say what we think, share what we know, and don't pretend to be something we're not.

Learn in public

We share our process, our mistakes, and our lessons. Building in the open makes us better and helps others along the way.
